## AI delcaration

Artificial intelligence, in the form of large language models, was used during the development of this project for the following purposes
- Writing a large part of our codebase. This includes
  - generating all the domain classes from a sql schema file
  - Implementing and improving functionality on the backend
  - making and improving views on the frontend
  - writing tests
  - writing documentation
  - generating mock data
  - identifying and fixing bugs
  - Writing interfaces for 
  - Writing parts of this wiki

The use of AI helped us save time, and allowed us to implement more functionality into the final product. I also helped us get a more complete end result with adequate testing and documentation.
It should be noted that AI was used on top of the foundation we built. AI did not decide what functionality we should implement, nor how we designed the system.

## How we used AI

We did not use spec-driven development, but rather used it as an assistant and peer programming partner where we were still mostly in the driver seat.


## Why we used AI

Due to time constraints and the huge task ahead of us we decided to use the tools at our disposal.
